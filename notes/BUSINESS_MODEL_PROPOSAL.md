# TradingSyncApp – Business Model Proposal

**Created**: February 11, 2026  
**Status**: Proposal for public launch

---

## Recommended path: Freemium from day one

### Why not fully free?
- You pay for every upload → hard to scale without revenue
- Free-only makes it harder to introduce paid later
- No clear signal of "real" users vs passive signups

### Why not paid-only?
- Low traction at launch
- Hard to get word-of-mouth and reviews
- Finance apps need trust before people pay

### Freemium balances:
- **Growth:** Free tier drives adoption and referrals
- **Revenue:** Paid tier covers costs and generates profit
- **Signals:** You see who uses it and who values it

---

## Suggested structure

### Phase 1: Launch (months 1–3)

| Tier | Limits | Goal |
|------|--------|------|
| **Free** | 2–3 accounts, 5 uploads/month | Acquisition, feedback |
| **Pro** | Unlimited accounts, unlimited uploads | Early revenue |

- **Pro price:** $2.99–3.99/month or $24.99/year
- **Free limits:** Enough to see value, not enough for heavy use
- **Goal:** 500–1,000 users, 2–5% conversion to paid

---

### Phase 2: Subscriptions (months 3–12)

- Improve onboarding and retention
- Add Pro-only features (e.g. history, projections, exports)
- Target **5–10% conversion** to paid
- Example: 5,000 users → 250–500 paid → ~$750–2,000/month

---

### Phase 3: Sponsors / affiliates (after year 1)

Sponsors care about:
- **Reach:** 10k+ monthly active users
- **Quality:** Users who actually invest
- **Context:** Portfolio app = relevant for trading/broker ads

**Potential partners:**
- **Trading 212** – affiliate/referral
- **Degiro, eToro, IBKR** – same idea
- **P2P platforms** – Mintos, Bondora, etc.
- **Insurers / banks** – if you add related products

**Typical deals:**
- **CPA:** $20–100 per referred user who trades
- **Rev share:** % of trading fees or deposits
- **Fixed sponsor fee:** €500–2,000/month for placement

---

## Should you start fully free?

**Yes, but only for a short launch period.**

| Approach | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Fully free for 1–2 months** | Fast growth, feedback, reviews | You pay all costs |
| **Freemium from day 1** | Revenue from day 1, sustainable | Slightly slower growth |

**Recommendation:** Start **freemium** with a generous free tier (e.g. 3 accounts, 5 uploads/month). If you want a "soft launch," you can go fully free for 4–6 weeks, then introduce limits and paid tier.

---

## Timeline (example)

| Phase | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|--------|
| **1. Launch** | months 1–3 | Freemium, feedback, fix bugs |
| **2. Iterate** | months 3–6 | Retention, Pro features, conversion |
| **3. Scale** | months 6–12 | Marketing, sponsors, affiliate talks |
| **4. Monetize** | year 2+ | Sponsors, partnerships, higher tiers |

---

## Cost context (per user)

- **API cost:** ~$0.10–0.30 per user (10 screenshots at avg $0.01–0.03 each)
- **Suggested price:** $2.99–4.99/month to cover costs + margin
- **1000 users:** ~$100–300 API cost; $2.99/mo × 1000 = $2,990 revenue (if all paid)

---

## Summary

- **Start:** Freemium (free + Pro)
- **Free:** 2–3 accounts, 5 uploads/month
- **Pro:** $2.99–3.99/month or ~$25/year
- **Later:** Sponsors (Trading 212, brokers, P2P) once you have 5–10k+ active users
- **Optional:** Fully free for 1–2 months at launch, then switch to freemium

Freemium gives you users and revenue from the start, and keeps the door open for sponsors once you have reach.
